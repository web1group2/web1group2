Fun weekend with friends from beneath

Join us for an unforgettable weekend filled with fun, fantasy, and fantastic underwater creatures! Our "Fun weekend with friends from beneath" event promises to be a magical experience for all ages.

1. Meet & Greet with Fantasy Sea Creatures

Date:
Saturday, June 15th
Time:
10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Get up close and personal with mythical sea creatures such as mermaids, sea dragons, and talking fish. Take photos, ask questions, and learn fascinating facts about these enchanting beings.

2. Underwater Treasure Hunt

Date:
Saturday, June 15th
Time:
1:00 PM - 3:00 PM

Embark on an adventurous treasure hunt in our specially designed underwater labyrinth. Find hidden treasures and win exciting prizes!

3. Mermaid Dance Party

Date:
Saturday, June 15th
Time:
6:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Dance the evening away with our mermaid friends. Enjoy live music, vibrant lights, and fun dance activities suitable for all ages.

Ticket Information

General Admission: $30 per person
Family Pack (4 tickets): $100
VIP Experience: $50 per person (includes exclusive meet & greet, front row seating for the parade, and a special gift bag)

Location

Fantasy Underwater Park
123 Ocean Drive, Atlantis City

How to Get There

By Car: Free parking available on site.
By Train: 10-minute walk from Atlantis City Station.
By Bus: Take the Blue Line to Ocean Drive stop.

What to Bring

Comfortable clothing and shoes, swimsuit for water activities, sunscreen and hats, camera or smartphone for photos, and a sense of adventure and excitement!
